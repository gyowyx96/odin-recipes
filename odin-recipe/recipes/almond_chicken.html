Almond Chicken

Tasty almond chicken japan style, easy to do with easy to get ingredients

2 persons ingredients

  • 500g chicken breasts
  • 120g peeled almonds
  • 60ml soy sauce
  • 2 water spoons
  • 1 little onion
  • 3cm fresh ginger
  • 3 seeds oil spoons
  • rise flour j.e.
  • 1 clove of garlic

STEPS

preparation time:30 minutes

  1. Toast the almond for a few minutes without oil until lightly browned
  2. Meanwhile cut the chicken breasts finely and pass them through the flour
  3. In a non-stick pan, better still in the wok, season the sliced ​​onions with the peeled and grated ginger and the crushed garlic clove. After a few minutes, add the chicken and brown it over high heat for 10 minutes, stirring often. A light crust should form.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and hot water, add the almonds and cook for another 2-3 minutes. The chicken with almonds is ready, serve it immediately.
