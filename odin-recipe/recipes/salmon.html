Pasta with salmon

Pasta with salmon, the easy way to eat fish

Three persons ingredients

  • butterfly pasta: 350g
  • fresh liquid cream 200g
  • golden onion 1/2
  • oil j.e.
  • smoked salmon 400g
  • brandy 50g
  • parsley j.e.
  • black pepper j.e.

STEPS

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil to cook the pasta and add salt to the boil. Cut the salmon into strips
  2. Clean and finely chop the onion and parsley
  3. Heat the oil in a pan and fry the onion, then add the salmon and turn up the heat
  4. After having saut√©ed the salmon for a few minutes, blend with the brandy and let it evaporate completely, then turn off the heat.
  5. At this point the water will have come to a boil, so add salt and cook the butterfly pasta for the time indicated on the package
  6. Meanwhile, pour the cream into the pan with the salmon, always with the heat off
  7. When the pasta is cooked, drain and transfer them directly to the pan
  8. Turn the heat back on and mix everything well to reduce the sauce
  9. Complete with the chopped parsley and a sprinkle of pepper
